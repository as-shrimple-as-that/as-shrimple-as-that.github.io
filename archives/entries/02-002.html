Entry Description: Part of a series of poems, this one being named "Barrier" It seems to be describing a longing to walk among the surface, and out of the water.
Above

He reaches out his hand.
And I reach out mine.
But...

A barrier.

My way... is blocked.
A force jolts through my arm
And I fall unconsious.


Such sorrow
Such misery
A love I'll never feel
And a hand
I'll never
Hold.
